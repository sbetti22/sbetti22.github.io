I am currently a STScI postdoctoral fellow at STScI. I got my Ph.D. in Astronomy at UMass Amherst, and my B.Sc. in Astrophysics at Haverford College in Pennsylvania. My current research focuses on accretion mechanism from circumstellar disks onto young brown dwarfs & planetary mass companions, and icy dust grains in circumstellar/debris disks around young stars.
