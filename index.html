I am currently a 4th year astronomy PhD candidate at UMass Amherst. I got my B.S. in Astrophysics at Haverford College in Pennsylvania. My current research focuses on accretion mechanism from circumstellar disks onto young brown dwarfs & planetary mass companions, and icy dust grains in circumstellar disks around young stars.
